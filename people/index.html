Current GROUP MEMBERS

Yaqian He is an Assistant Professor in Department of Geography at University of Central Arkansasa. Before joining UCA, She worked as a postdoc research fellow in Dr. Justin Mankin’s research group in the Department of Geography at Dartmouth College. She got her PhD degree in Geography at the Department of Geology and Geography, West Virginia University in 2018. During her PhD, she was supervised by Dr. Eungul Lee and working closely with Dr. Tim Warner. Before coming to the US, She got her master degree in Cartology and Geographic Information System in Dr. Yanchen Bo’s lab at Beijing Normal University in 2013 and bachelor in Surveying Engineering at China University of Mining and Technology in 2010. Check her CV.

Faith Vire is a first-year graduate student pursuing a master's of Geographic Information Systems at the University of Central Arkansas. She graduated in December 2021 with a bachelor's degree in geography, also from UCA. Faith is currently studying temporal changes in crop phenology in Arkansas with Dr. He. When she is not conducting research, she enjoys reading horror novels and caring for her two cats.
