CURRENT GROUP MEMBERS

Yaqian He is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Geosciences at the University of Arkansas, Fayetteville. Before joining UARK, she worked as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Geography at the University of Central Arkansas from 2020 to 2024. Before that, she worked as a postdoc research fellow in Dr. Justin Mankin’s research group in the Department of Geography at Dartmouth College. She also worked closely with Dr. Jonathan Chipman. She got her PhD in Geography at the Department of Geology and Geography, West Virginia University in 2018, supervised by Dr. Eungul Lee, and worked closely with Dr. Tim Warner. Before coming to the US, she received her master's in Cartology and GIS at Dr. Yanchen Bo’s lab at Beijing Normal University in 2013 and her bachelor's in Surveying Engineering at China University of Mining and Technology in 2010.

Faith Vire is a research assistant working on detecting crop phenology and its relationship with climate. She earned her master's degree in Geographic Information Systems at the University of Central Arkansas. Outside of research, she enjoys reading horror novels and caring for her two cats.
